NEWBORN FOUND FLOATING AT SEA THE body of a newborn baby girl with an umbilical cord was found floating at sea in the waters off Zone 5, Km. 11 in Barangay Sasa, Davao City. The Sasa Police Station received the alarm about the infant at 5:50 a.m. Thursday, September 13. The Mobile Patrol 25 led by SPO3 Dominick Patajo and PO1 Perlito Buscagan responded immediately to the alarm. Upon their arrival in the area, the police found the baby floating in the waters at the back of the Coast Guard Sub-Station in Sasa. The body of the baby was brought to the Rivera Funeral Parlor in Km. 12, Sasa. The police are now conducting a thorough investigation on the incident.
