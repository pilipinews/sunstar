FIRE HITS ASIAN GRAINS WAREHOUSE IN CEBU

PHOTO: https://www.sunstar.com.ph/uploads/images/2018/07/15/68990.jpg

FIRE is in progress at a warehouse owned by industrial flour milling company Asian Grains Corporation in the City of Naga, about 20 kilometers south of Cebu City.

As of 8 p.m. Sunday, July 15, the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) in Naga has declared a general alarm, which means that all available fire trucks in Cebu are required to respond.

VIDEO: https://www.facebook.com/superbalitacebu/videos/1709514985813920/

Asian Grains is a sister company of Ricor Mills Corporation and Cagayan Corn Products Corporation. (SUNSTAR PHILIPPINES)
